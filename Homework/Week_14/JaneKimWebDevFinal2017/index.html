About

Junction Studio provides painting and pottery classes of all ages. Anyone can be an artist, and Junction Studio follows its motto of "Anyone can do it" to the fullest. Although we are relativly new, as we first opened in 2017, we make sure not to compromise enjoyment in our short hour long classes. It is important to us that you have fun while learning different art techniques. We currently have two locations. Our first studio was opened in Manhattan in 2016. We have been lucky enough to open up our second studio in Brooklyn in 2017.

Classes are offered 7 days a week, with a bunch of one and two day classes in specific techniques and a bunch of 6 week project based classes. Private lessons are available for individuals or for groups, especially good for specialized training. We are also available for project consulting.
